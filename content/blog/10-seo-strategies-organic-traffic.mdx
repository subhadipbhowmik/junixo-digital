Search engine optimisation has never been more competitive — or more rewarding. Brands that get SEO right generate millions in organic revenue without paying for every click.

Here are the 10 strategies we use at Junixo to consistently drive organic growth for our clients.

## 1. Build a Topical Authority Framework

Google increasingly rewards websites that demonstrate **deep expertise** on a subject, not just individual high-ranking pages.

### What This Means in Practice

Rather than writing random blog posts targeting random keywords, build a structured content hub:

- A main "pillar" page covering the broad topic
- Supporting cluster posts that go deep on subtopics
- Internal links connecting everything together

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
  Start by mapping every question your target customer asks around your core
  topic. Group them into clusters. This becomes your content calendar for the
  next 6 months.
</Callout>

## 2. Fix Your Technical SEO Foundation

You can't rank a slow, broken website. Technical SEO is the foundation everything else sits on.

### Core Technical Priorities

- **Core Web Vitals**: LCP under 2.5s, FID under 100ms, CLS under 0.1
- **Crawl budget**: Ensure Googlebot can access your important pages
- **Canonical tags**: Eliminate duplicate content issues
- **Schema markup**: Help Google understand your content

## 3. Master Search Intent Matching

The single biggest reason good content fails to rank: **intent mismatch**.

### The 4 Types of Search Intent

- **Informational**: "How to do X"
- **Navigational**: "Junixo Digital pricing"
- **Commercial**: "Best SEO agency London"
- **Transactional**: "Buy SEO services"

Every page on your site should target one clear intent. Match your content format to that intent.

## 4. Build Links That Actually Move Rankings

Not all links are equal. A single link from a relevant, authoritative site can outperform 100 low-quality links.

### Our Link Building Playbook

- Digital PR: Create data-led studies journalists want to cite
- Resource link building: Get onto industry resource pages
- HARO / Qwoted: Answer journalist requests as an expert
- Broken link building: Replace dead links with your content

## 5. Optimise for Featured Snippets

Featured snippets appear above the first organic result and can double your click-through rate.

<Callout type="info" title="Did You Know?">
  70% of all Google searches are long-tail queries. Yet most brands only target
  short, high-competition keywords. This is a massive opportunity hiding in
  plain sight.
</Callout>

## 6. Leverage Entity SEO

Google understands **entities** — people, places, brands, and concepts — not just keywords. Building your brand as a recognised entity gives you a lasting competitive advantage.

## 7. Create Content That Earns Backlinks Naturally

Content that gets shared and linked to tends to be original research, ultimate guides, free tools, or counter-intuitive opinions backed by data.

## 8. Prioritise Long-Tail Keywords

Long-tail keywords (4+ words) have lower competition and higher buyer intent. Start there and build up.

## 9. Refresh and Repurpose Existing Content

Updating old content is one of the fastest ways to recover lost rankings and improve organic traffic.

### Content Refresh Checklist

- Update statistics and dates
- Add new sections covering topics you missed
- Improve internal links
- Add schema markup if missing
- Compress images for speed

## 10. Track, Measure, and Iterate

What you don't measure, you can't improve. Track organic sessions, keyword rankings, and organic conversion rate monthly.
